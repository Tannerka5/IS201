My Wonderful, Fantastic Life All About Me!

I am currently an undergraduate student at Brigham Young University studying Business Management with a minor in Enviornmental Science. I will be graduating in 2025 and hope to find a career in business consulting in order to help businesses become more sustainable and environmentally friendly in their projects and current operations.

Born in Merced, California, I have lived in many different states all of which gave me many unique life experiences. I love my direct and extended family and a few of the things that I am passionate about can be found below!

Best workout for your body and your brain Rock Climbing

Definitely my biggest passion, there is just something about climbing and getting to the top of a hard route. I started climbing years ago but began climbing regularly at the beginning of summer 2021. Climbing can be scary or tiring, but that's exactly what I look forward to. If I ever have any free time in my day, I always try to go climbing, whether it is outside or even at the indoor gym. Bouldering is my favorite type of climbing!

Seeing the Outdoors Hiking

I absolutely love the outdoors and when I'm hiking, I'm able to take life slowly and simply appreciate the beauty of the Earth around me. Whether I'm hiking with my friends, family, dog, or just by myself, I always have a fantastic time being able to enjoy nature.

Going Around the World Travelling

One of my strongest desires is to travel the Earth and experience different areas and cultures. A few places on my bucket list are the islands of Greece, Southern Japan, Paris, and the coast of Spain. I also love food, so being able to travel to different areas of the world would also allow me to taste the wide variety of different cuisine!
